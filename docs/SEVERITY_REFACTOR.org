* Overview

** Goal
Refactor notification system to pair alert timing with urgency levels as atomic units (cons cells), eliminating possibility of mismatch bugs.

** Why This Change?
Current design has two separate variables that can get out of sync:
- ~chime-alert-time~ - list of minutes before event to notify
- ~chime-alert-severity~ - single severity for ALL notifications

Problem: Cannot have different urgency for "60 min before" vs "at event time".

** Solution
Replace with single variable containing cons cells pairing time with severity:
#+begin_src elisp
;; OLD (separate, can mismatch)
(setq chime-alert-time '(10 5 0))
(setq chime-alert-severity 'medium)  ; applies to all 3

;; NEW (paired, can't mismatch)
(setq chime-alert-intervals
      '((10 . medium)    ; 10 min before: medium urgency
        (5  . medium)    ; 5 min before: medium urgency
        (0  . high)))    ; at event: high urgency
#+end_src

** Breaking Changes
This is a BREAKING CHANGE for alpha users. Since we're in alpha, making this breaking change now before wider adoption.

* Data Flow Analysis

** Current Flow
#+begin_example
chime-alert-time (list of numbers)
    ↓
chime--gather-info stores in event.intervals
    ↓
chime--notifications pairs with timestamps
    ↓
chime--check-event creates notification messages
    ↓
chime--notify uses global chime-alert-severity
#+end_example

** New Flow
#+begin_example
chime-alert-intervals (list of (time . severity) cons)
    ↓
chime--gather-info stores cons cells in event.intervals
    ↓
chime--notifications pairs with timestamps, preserves severity
    ↓
chime--check-event creates (message . severity) pairs
    ↓
chime--notify receives severity as parameter
#+end_example

* Example Transformations

** Before
#+begin_src elisp
;; Configuration
(setq chime-alert-time '(60 30 10 0))
(setq chime-alert-severity 'medium)

;; Internal representation
'((times . timestamps)
  (intervals . (60 30 10 0))
  ...)

;; Notification - all same severity
(chime--notify "Meeting in 60 minutes")  ; uses global 'medium
(chime--notify "Meeting in 10 minutes")  ; uses global 'medium
(chime--notify "Meeting now")            ; uses global 'medium
#+end_src

** After
#+begin_src elisp
;; Configuration
(setq chime-alert-intervals
      '((60 . low)     ; early warning: low urgency
        (30 . low)
        (10 . medium)  ; getting close: medium urgency
        (0  . high)))  ; at event: high urgency

;; Internal representation
'((times . timestamps)
  (intervals . ((60 . low) (30 . low) (10 . medium) (0 . high)))
  ...)

;; Notification - severity per interval
(chime--notify "Meeting in 60 minutes" 'low)     ; low urgency
(chime--notify "Meeting in 10 minutes" 'medium)  ; medium urgency
(chime--notify "Meeting now" 'high)              ; high urgency
#+end_src

* Migration Notes for Users

** Alpha Breaking Change Notice
#+begin_example
BREAKING CHANGE in v0.7.0: Alert configuration format changed

Old format (no longer supported):
  (setq chime-alert-time '(10 5 0))
  (setq chime-alert-severity 'medium)

New format (required):
  (setq chime-alert-intervals '((10 . medium) (5 . medium) (0 . high)))

Migration guide:
1. Combine your alert times with desired severity
2. Replace chime-alert-time with chime-alert-intervals
3. Remove chime-alert-severity (no longer used)
#+end_example
